Crispy Bacon

Description

Perfect for breakfast!

Crispy and salty, just like you like it!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of thick sliced bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C.
  2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  3. Arrange the bacon in a single layer on the prepared sheet with the edges touching or slightly overlapping.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven to desired degree of doneness, 10 to 15 minutes.
  5. Remove bacon from the baking sheet with tongs or a fork, and drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
